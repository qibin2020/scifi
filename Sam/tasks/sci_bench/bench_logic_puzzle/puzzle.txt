There are 5 houses in a row, numbered 1 (far left) to 5 (far right).
Each house has a unique color, is occupied by a person of a unique nationality,
and each person keeps a unique pet.

Colors:  Red, Blue, Green, Yellow, White
Nations: Brit, Swede, Dane, German, Norwegian
Pets:    Dog, Cat, Bird, Fish, Horse

Constraints:
1. The Brit lives in the red house.
2. The Swede keeps a dog.
3. The Norwegian lives in house 1.
4. The green house is house 5.
5. The blue house is immediately to the left of the red house.
6. The person in house 2 keeps a horse.
7. The Dane lives in house 2.
8. The German keeps a bird.
9. House 1 is yellow.
10. The person in house 3 keeps fish.
11. The German lives in house 4.

# ANSWER: House1=Yellow,Norwegian,Cat; House2=Blue,Dane,Horse; House3=Red,Brit,Fish; House4=White,German,Bird; House5=Green,Swede,Dog
